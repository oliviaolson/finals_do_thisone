    • How to play!

    Adventure through a 40 space Candyland gameboard. You will meet a character every 10 spaces including Mr. Mint, Mr. Jolly, Queen Frostine, and Gloppy. These characters can be good or evil, good ones are able to boost you to the next character. Evil characters are able to kill you if answer incorrectly. At each character you will be asked a random trivia question.
